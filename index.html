About Us

Welcome to Chipsvilla, where passion meets flavor in every crispy bite. Founded in 2023 by Boluwagbe, we bring you the finest plantain chips inspired by our cultural heritage. Sourced from local farmers, our plaintains are sustainably grown and hand-picked for quality. We offera variety of flavors, from classic salted to sugar and honey coated and also spicy, all made with natural ingredients and no artificial additives. Our chips are gluten-free, vegan and packed with nutrients. Thank you for choosing us, enjoy the taste of tradition and the crunch of quality in every bite
